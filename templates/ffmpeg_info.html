Guide for FFMPEG commands

General guidelines:
  • Convert videos to Matroska container with .mkv suffix
  • Convert video stream to FFV1
  • Convert audio stream to FLAC or LPCM
  • Avoid whitespaces in output files

    • Video stream to FFV1 and Audio stream to FLAC:
    ffmpeg -i Pipilotti_Rist.mov -vcodec ffv1 -level 3 -g 1 -slicecrc 1 -context 1 -acodec flac Pipilotti_Rist_FFV1_FLAC.mkv
    Video stream to FFV1 and TWO Audio streams to two different FLAC streams:
    ffmpeg -i Michael_Smith-Secret_horror_DV.mov -map 0:0 -map 0:1 -map 0:2 -c:v:0 ffv1 -level 3 -g 1 -slicecrc 1 -context 1 -c:a:0 flac -c:a:1 flac Michael_Smith-Secret_FFV1_2xFLAC.mkv
    Video stream to FFV1 and COMBINE two different FLAC audio streams into one audio stream:
    ffmpeg -i Michael_Smith-Secret_horror_DV-FFV1_FLACx2.mkv -filter_complex amix=inputs=2 -c:v copy -c:a flac Michael_Smith-Secret_horror_DV-FFV1_FLACx1.mkv
    Only Audio stream to FLAC in Matroska .mkv container:
    ffmpeg -i Pipilotti_Rist.mov -vn -acodec flac Pipilotti_Rist_Only_FLAC.mkv
    Video stream to FFV1 and Audio stream to PCM:
    ffmpeg -i Pipilotti_Rist.mov -vcodec ffv1 -level 3 -g 1 -slicecrc 1 -context 1 -acodec pcm_s24be Pipilotti_Rist_FFV1_PCM.mkv

    Input files with whitespaces / Video stream to FFV1 and Audio stream to FLAC:
    ffmpeg -i "Juha van Ingen - Disintegrator.mov" -vcodec ffv1 -level 3 -g 1 -slicecrc 1 -context 1 -acodec flac Juha_van_Ingen_Disintegrator_FFV1_FLAC.mkv
    Input files with whitespaces / Video stream to FFV1 and Audio stream to PCM:
    ffmpeg -i "Juha van Ingen - Disintegrator.mov" -vcodec ffv1 -level 3 -g 1 -slicecrc 1 -context 1 -acodec pcm_s24be Juha_van_Ingen_Disintegrator_FFV1_PCM.mkv

    Message digest calculation for video FRAME checksum:
    ffmpeg -loglevel error -i Pipilotti_Rist_FFV1_FLAC.mkv -map 0:v -f md5 -
